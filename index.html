The Technology

HAPS
Mesh

A network of solar-powered UAVs operating at stratospheric altitude. Each aircraft locks position above a target region and beams high-speed internet directly below — covering 500km with a single node. No cables. No towers. No fuel.

How It Works
01
Ascent
The aircraft climbs to stratospheric altitude — above weather, above turbulence — where conditions are stable and solar irradiance is constant.
02
Station Keep
AI-driven controls maintain precise position over the target region against jet stream winds. Solar energy harvested by day charges batteries for continuous night operations.
03
Broadcast
High-speed internet beamed directly to a 500km coverage zone below. Multiple nodes mesh together to form seamless, redundant, continent-scale connectivity.
